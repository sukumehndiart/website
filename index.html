About The Artist

Meet Suku

Hi — I'm Sakshi (Suku). I specialize in creating beautiful bridal, Arabic, and Indo-Western mehndi designs that blend tradition with modern sensibilities. With a passion for intricate patterns and a keen eye for detail, I create personalized designs that tell your unique story.

I work exclusively with natural, high-quality henna to ensure safe application and long-lasting, rich stains that will beautifully complement your celebration.
